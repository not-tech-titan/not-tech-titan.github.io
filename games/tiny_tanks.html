Tiny Tanks

Watch out for that mine!

Tiny Tanks reimagines the classic Wii Play "Tanks" minigame as a fully-featured experience. Players control miniature tanks in diverse arenas with destructible obstacles, power-ups, and interactive hazards. The game leverages dynamic AI systems to create challenging solo experiences. Controls are responsive, movement and shooting are finely tuned, and arenas are designed for strategy as well as chaotic fun. Tiny Tanks emphasizes fast-paced, skill-driven combat while maintaining a playful, accessible aesthetic that captures the charm of the original mini-game and expands it into a full-fledged, replayable title.

Controls

  • WASD – Move
  • Mouse – Aim / Shoot
  • Space – Place Mine
  • Esc – Pause
