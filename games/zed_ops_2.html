Zed Ops Arcade 2

The horror continues...

Zed Ops Arcade 2 is a wave-based zombie survival shooter and the sequel to the original Zed Ops Arcade. Built in Unreal Engine 5, the game expands on the fast-paced survival formula with larger arenas, improved visuals, and deeper progression between rounds. Players must survive increasingly difficult waves of undead while managing ammo, positioning, and timing. Weapons can now be purchased directly from the walls, allowing players to quickly rearm and adapt their strategy as the difficulty escalates. Choosing when to upgrade gear, conserve resources, or push forward becomes critical to surviving longer runs. The game emphasizes tight combat, tactical movement, and escalating pressure, rewarding players who can stay calm while the battlefield fills with enemies. Enhanced lighting, particle effects, and atmosphere create a tense environment where every wave feels more dangerous than the last. Zed Ops Arcade 2 builds on the foundation of the original while delivering a more refined, modern survival experience.

Controls

  • WASD – Move
  • Mouse – Aim / Shoot
  • Space – Jump
  • Esc – Pause
