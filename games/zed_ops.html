Zed Ops Arcade

Never stop running!

Zed Ops Arcade is a infinite zombie shooter survival inspired by Doom and Goldeneye 007. Players face relentless hordes of undead, navigating maps filled with tight corridors, strategic chokepoints, an abundance of upgrades, and no way out. The game emphasizes tactical gameplay, requiring players to manage limited resources, coordinate movement, and plan ahead to survive. Each match challenges players to adapt to evolving threats, rewarding skill, planning, and rapid decision-making. The dark, atmospheric environments are enhanced with lighting and particle effects to immerse players in a tense survival experience.

Controls

  • WASD – Move
  • Mouse – Aim / Shoot
  • Space – Jump
  • Shift – Run
  • Escape – Pause
