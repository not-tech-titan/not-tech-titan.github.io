Zed Ops: Rezurection

Fetch me their souls!

Zed Ops: Rezurection is a wave-based survival shooter inspired by the Nintendo DS port of Call of Duty: Black Ops Zombies. Players face relentless hordes of undead, navigating maps filled with tight corridors, strategic chokepoints, an abundance of upgrades, and no way out. The game emphasizes tactical gameplay, requiring players to manage limited resources, coordinate movement, and plan ahead to survive. Built using the Tech Titan Engine, Zed Ops provides scalable AI behavior, sophisticated pathfinding, and responsive combat mechanics. Each match challenges players to adapt to evolving threats, rewarding skill, planning, and rapid decision-making. The dark, atmospheric environments are enhanced with lighting and particle effects to immerse players in a tense survival experience.

Controls

  • WASD – Move
  • Mouse – Aim / Shoot
  • Space – Jump
  • Esc – Pause
