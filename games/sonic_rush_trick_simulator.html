Sonic Rush Trick Simulator

Yes! Ok! Yes! Ok!

Sonic Rush Trick Simulator is a basic trick simulator made in Scratch inspired by Sonic Rush's trick system. Do stylish tricks, rack up points, gather insane score multipliers, and try to beat your high score! The game emphasizes stylish gameplay, requiring players to carefully chain tricks together to maximize their score.

Controls

  • Space/Click/Tap Screen – Do Trick
