Space Storm

Navigate the void and blast anything in your path!

Space Storm is a retro-inspired 2D space shooter with Vectrex inspired graphics. Players pilot a nimble starfighter through increasingly challenging waves of enemy ships, asteroids, and environmental hazards. Each level emphasizes fast reflexes, strategic positioning, and skillful use of ship abilities. The game showcases physics-based movement, smooth animations, and responsive controls, providing an engaging arcade experience. Whether dodging bullets, chaining combos, or upgrading your ship, every run rewards precision and timing. Space Storm combines nostalgia with modern gameplay polish, creating a satisfying and visually dynamic space combat experience.

Controls

  • WASD – Move
  • Mouse – Aim / Shoot
  • Space – Boost
  • Esc – Pause
