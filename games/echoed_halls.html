Echoed Halls

Will you be the first to escape?

Echoed Halls is a procedurally generated first-person dungeon crawler focused on exploration, discovery, and atmospheric tension. Each playthrough presents unique dungeon layouts, enemy placements, and loot, ensuring no two runs feel the same. Players traverse twisting corridors, hidden chambers, and ominous halls, encountering environmental puzzles, traps, and AI-driven adversaries. The game implements a Drunkard's Walk algorithm for dungeon generation, alongside careful memory and performance optimization to maintain smooth gameplay. Audio and lighting work in tandem to immerse players, creating a sense of mystery and suspense at every turn. Echoed Halls challenges both strategic thinking and reflexes, rewarding exploration and careful planning with powerful rewards and hidden secrets.

Controls

  • Left Stick - Move
  • Drag Right – Look Around
  • Left Button – Attack
  • Right Button – Switch Weapon
